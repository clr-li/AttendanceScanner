Highlights

Unique QR Codes

Each group gets a unique QR code that members can scan to join. Everyone also gets a unique QR code linked to their Google Account which can be scanned to take attendance. Unify your attendance under one login!

Multiple Admins

You can delegate roles and privileges to other members as desired. Admins have access to a QR code scanner, event creation, and can view/update member attendance in both table form and as quick statistics.

Calendar View

Easily browse past and upcoming events from your groups. Create and view recurring events. Edit previous attendance records as needed.

Participate in many groups

View your past attendance and upcoming events, manage which groups you participate in, and notify group admins if you know you'll be late/absent.
