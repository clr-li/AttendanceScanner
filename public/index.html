Features

Unique QR Codes

Each business gets a unique QR code which others can scan to join. Everyone that creates an account also gets a unique QR code which can be scanned by a business to get their attendance taken.

Multiple Admins

Busiensses can have more than one admin. Admins have access to a QR code scanner, an event manager, and a calendar view of everyone's attendance.
